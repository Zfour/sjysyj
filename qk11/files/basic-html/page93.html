               18         我国当代景观照明的舞台化探析 *


                          Analysis of Contemporary Theatrical Landscape Lighting





                          赵中建 Zhao Zhongjian
                          南京航空航天大学，南京211106 （Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics，211106
                          Nanjing )




               摘要：本文选择改革开放以来城市夜景照明为研究对象，尝试着分析和概括了当代景观照明的“舞台化”的三个属性、景观照明舞
               台化的三个运行机制以及景观照明舞台化的三个应用策略，期冀对我国当代景观照明的舞台化学理研究有所助益，且为今后的景
               观照明研究提供一种借鉴和参考。
               关键词：景观照明；舞台照明；机制；应用
               Abstract:  The research object of this paper is the design of city night lighting in China after the 1980s. This paper analyzes the stage
               attributes of landscape lighting, the operational mechanism of stage lighting and applications. It aims to provide suggestions for city night
               lighting design and construction in China.
               Key words: landscape lighting； stage illumination；  operation mechanism； application
