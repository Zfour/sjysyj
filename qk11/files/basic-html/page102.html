               19         数字游牧民服务设计研究



                          Research on the Digital Nomad Service Design






                          刘琳 LIU LIN
                          武汉理工大学，湖北武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan Hubei）




               摘要：本文以用户体验为中心为数字游牧民提出针对性的服务设计，解决目标用户触点问题，完善数字游牧民服务设计体系。运
               用文献分析法、材料分析法、模拟服务场景的服务蓝图分析法，梳理出数字游牧民在服务设计流程中各阶段的触点，划分现存问
               题和优化目标。对比目前相关服务设计发展情况，总结现存问题和盲点，结合实际案例提供解决方法和探讨解决思路。为数字游
               牧民提供服务设计和设计方案，包括徽章设计、APP设计，以提升用户在服务流程中的体验，优化服务设计体系。
               关键词：数字游牧民；用户体验；服务设计
               Abstract: The paper Focuses on user experience as the center for digital nomads to propose targeted service design to solve the problem
               of target user contact and improve the digital nomad's service design system. It uses the literature analysis, material analysis, and service
               blueprint analysis to simulate the service scene. It sorts out the contacts of digital nomads in various stages of the service design process,
               divides existing problems and optimizes the objectives, compares the current development of related service design, summarizes existing
               problems and blind spots, and discusses solutions by combining practical cases. Digital nomad service design meets market demand, provides
               non-face-to-face instant information, proposes service design strategies and design solutions, simplifies the process of digital nomads to
               obtain information, and can effectively solve user contact problems, thereby integrating and optimizing service design systems.
               Key words: digital nomad；user experience；service design
