               22         日用陶瓷 —— 茶具产品语意设计研究 *


                          Study on Domestic Ceramic  Semantics Design of Tea Sets







                          韩军 HAN Jun    舒贝宁 Shu Beining
                          武汉工程大学，武汉430205  （Wuhan Institute of Technology，430205 Wuhan ）



               摘要：陶瓷艺术最初是以日用陶瓷产品的形式起源的，但在陶瓷制作工艺日臻完美的背后，存在着一个无法回避的问题，那就是
               日用陶瓷产品的设计严重滞后，这已成为制约这个行业健康发展的重要因素。本文通过分析日用陶瓷产品的特征，以茶具为切入
               点，提出以语意设计角度入手，提升日用陶瓷产品的内涵，分别从模拟法、叙事法和寓意法进行具体阐述，为日用陶瓷产品设计
               提供有效的参考。
               关键词：日用陶瓷；茶具；语意设计；内涵
               Abstract:  Ceramic art originated from domestic ceramic products, but when the production technique has advanced much, the design of
               domestic ceramics has lagged behind and become a hindrance to the development of the industry. This paper takes tea sets as the case and
               analyzes the characteristics of the domestic ceramic to find ways to enhance the connotation of domestic ceramic in semantic design angle
               from the aspects of simulating, narrating and allegory.
               Key words: domestic ceramics；tea set；semantic design；connotation
