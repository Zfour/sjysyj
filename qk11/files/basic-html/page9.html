               02         传统几何装饰纹样意蕴在家具设计中的应用


                          Application of Geometric Decorative Patterns in Furniture
                          Design





                          李君华 LI Junhua    胡荣 Hu Rong
                          武汉工程大学， 武汉 430205（Wuhan Institute of Technology，430205 Wuhan）



               摘要：中国古代家具以其独特的榫卯工艺结构、简洁雅致的造型、硬木材质、细腻的表现手法，以及深厚的文化底蕴和艺术特色
               体现了中国一脉相承的民族文化，对于现代家具设计具有极高的借鉴和鉴赏价值。总结家具史上几何装饰纹样发展变化特征，通
               过分析传统几何装饰纹样与现代功能审美的契合点，挖掘迄今问题的解决方法，探索传统几何装饰纹样在现代家具设计中的应用。
               最后运用传统几何装饰纹样为中国现代家具设计实践寻找自身特色带来启发，探索中国现代家具设计如何通过应用传统几何纹样
               发展自身特色，同时也符合简洁实用、形式追随功能的现代审美观。
               关键词：几何形态；传统意蕴；家具；现代设计
               Abstract:  As a necessity of life, furniture bears the information of times, politics, economy and culture. Ancient Chinese furniture, with its
               unique mortise and tenon structure, simple and elegant modeling, hardwood materials, exquisite presentation techniques, as well as profound
               cultural heritage and artistic characteristics, embodies the national culture of China, which has high reference and appreciation value for
               modern furniture design. This paper summarizes the development and change of geometric decorative patterns in the history of furniture,
               analyzes the coincidence between traditional geometric decorative patterns and modern functional aesthetics, and explores solutions
               to problems so far as well as the application of traditional geometric decorative patterns in modern furniture design. Finally, the use of
               traditional geometric decorative patterns brings inspiration to the practice of modern furniture design in China to find its own characteristics,
               and explore how Chinese modern furniture design can develop its own characteristics through the application of traditional geometric
               patterns, which also conforms to the simple and practical modern aesthetics with form following function.
               Key words:  geometric；traditional implication；furniture；modern design
