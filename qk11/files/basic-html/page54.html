               11         沉静永恒


                          —— 论 《戴珍珠耳环的少女》  的视觉色彩与情感


                          Being Quiet and Eternal： On Visual Color and Sentiment of
                          Girl with a Pearl Earring




                          谢小涵 XIE Xiaohan
                          武汉理工大学，湖北武汉 430070（Wuhan University of Technology，430070 Wuhan Hubei）



               摘要：《戴珍珠耳环的少女》这幅历史名作中少女回眸时神秘的微笑，让不少人为之着迷，其故事还被拍成同名电影，电影虽是虚
               构，却很好地传达了维米尔对于艺术的追求精神以及画作想表达的内在情感。本文结合画作和同名电影，分析了维米尔如何运用
               艺术创作手法形成视觉色彩、表达情感内容、创造艺术内涵。
               关键词：《戴珍珠耳环的少女》；视觉色彩；艺术情感；表现手法
               Abstract: This historical painting of Girl with a Pearl Earring is deeply loved by the public, especially the mysterious smile when the girl
               returns, which makes many people fascinated. The film, although fictional, can also reflect Vermeer’s spirit of pursuing art, better understand
               the inner feelings that the painting wants to express, and analyze the artistic creation of Vermeer through the combination of painting and
               film. The technique forms its visual color and emotional content, reflecting a highly profound artistic connotation.
               Key words: Girl with a Pearl Earring； visual color； artistic emotion； expression technique
