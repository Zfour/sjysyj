               06         再创插画艺术中的甲骨文文化 *


                          Re-creating Oracle Culture in Illustration Art







                          孙慧良 SUN Huiliang    杜鑫 DU Xin    王月 WANG Yue
                          东北林业大学，黑龙江哈尔滨 150040（Northeast Forestry University，150040  Harbin
                          Heilongjiang ）



               摘要：甲骨文是指刻在龟甲或兽骨上的文字,主要用于占卜和记录。甲骨文被认为是当代汉字的原型，也是中国古代最早的成熟文
               字。甲骨文对于钻研和追索人类文明的历史有着极其深刻的意义。甲骨文合文字体偏多、图画色彩浓厚、异体字较多等特点，使
               其成为插画设计中的重要设计资源，将插画设计与甲骨文结合可以将甲骨文文化及其魅力传达出来，再现甲骨文的艺术感染力。
               关键词：甲骨文；插画设计；创新
               Abstract:  Oracle-bone inscriptions are written on tortoise shells or animal bones, mainly used for divination and recording. Oracle bone
               inscriptions are believed to be the archetype of contemporary Chinese characters. It is also the most mature writing in ancient China. Oracle-
               bone inscriptions are of great significance for studying and tracing the history of human civilization. Oracle has a long history. The features of
               multiple characters, strong picture color and many different characters are important design resources in illustration design. The combination
               of illustration design and oracle bone inscriptions can express the oracle culture and its own charm and reproduce the artistic appeal of oracle
               culture.
               Key words: oracle；illustration design；innovation
