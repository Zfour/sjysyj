               04         阳新布贴中同构图形的分析 *


                          Analysis of the Isomorphic Graph of Yangxin Cloth Paste







                                                   2
                                1
                          胡舒娟  HU Shujuan    石小波  SHI Xiaobo
                          1. 武汉设计工程学院，武汉 430205（Wuhan Institute of Design and Sciences，430205 Wuhan）
                          2. 华中农业大学，武汉 430070（Hua Zhong Agriculture University，430070 Wuhan）




               摘要：作为国家级非物质文化遗产的阳新布贴在图案上有着鲜明的特点，同构图形就是其一。同构图形是一种特殊的图形处理手
               法，在中国有着极为悠久的历史。通过对阳新布贴中最具有代表性的同构图形进行分析，发现其同构图形的特点，找到使用同构
               手法的原因，从而更深入地研究阳新布贴的艺术特点，并尝试为其寻找新的创新点。
               关键词：阳新布贴；同构；图案；装饰
               Abstract:  As a national intangible cultural heritage, Yangxin cloth has distinct characteristics in the pattern, isomorphic graphics being
               one of them. Isomorphic graph is a special method of graphic processing. It has a very long history in China. This paper analyzes the most
               representative isomorphic figure of Yangxin cloth paste, finds out the characteristics of its isomorphic figure, and the reasons for using
               isomorphic technique, so as to further study its artistic characteristics and find new ways for its innovation.
               Key words: Yangxin cloth patch；isomorphism；pattern； decoration
