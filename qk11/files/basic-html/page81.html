               16         当“印迹”蕴藏“书香”*


                          —— 凸版画艺术语式融入手工书装设计的实验性探索


                          When the imprint contains the fragrance of books：An
                          experimental exploration of the use of convex print art
                          style in manual book design






                          胡超 HU Chao    马飞飞 MA Feifei
                              1
                                              2
                          1. 江西农业大学，江西南昌 330045（Jiangxi Agricultural University，330045 Nanchang Jiangxi）
                          2. 普迪学校，浙江宁波 315031（Pudi School，315031 Ningbo Zhejiang）



               摘要：“手工书装设计”主要研究书籍生产过程中的装潢设计工作。人们对纸媒书籍的特殊情感，触发装帧手工书的热潮，精致素
               雅的手工书深受大众欢迎，更被书籍爱好者收藏。正因如此，国内高校“书籍装帧设计”课程教学开始手工书装设计技能的培养。
               以高校艺术院系“书籍装帧设计”课程为实验载体，以版画创意思维为逻辑构建，注重凸版画艺术语言与内外形式，在图文媒介
              “纸媒书籍”装帧设计中的形式演化与拓展，实验性地以版画的本体属性“肌理与印痕”所传递的视知觉为研究切入点，深入剖析手
               工书装工艺中的造型设计、结构设计及外观设计这3个构成因素及其相关概念、起源及发展过程。
               关键词：手工书装；凸版画；刻痕印迹；质料触感；艺术语式
               Abstract:  The course Handmade Book Design is a core course of art design in terms of studying the decoration design work in the
               process of book production in colleges and universities. People become interested in book binding because of special feelings on the paper
               books which are collected by book enthusiasts. The course Handmade Book Design in domestic colleges and universities aims to cultivate
               students’ skills in book binding design. It constructs the creative thinking of printmaking, focuses on the form evolution and expansion of the
               engraving art language and internal and external forms in the graphic paper book binding design, and experimentally takes the Noumenon
               attribute of printmaking. The entry point of research is the visual perception transmitted by the skin texture and the imprint, then in-depth
               analysis of the composition factors and their related concepts, origins and development process in three aspects of the design, structure design
               and appearance design of the manual book-binding process.
               Key words: handmade book；convex print；engraved imprint；material touch；art language
