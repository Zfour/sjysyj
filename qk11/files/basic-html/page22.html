               05       “充实之谓美”对当代设计艺术的启示


                          Fullness as Beauty and Its Inspiration for Modern Design
                          Art







                          何蓉莹 HE Rongying
                          西南大学，重庆 400715（Southwest  University，400715 Chongqing）




               摘要：“充实之谓美”是孟子美学思想中的一个重要命题，不仅仅是关于人格美，孟子还以此将人对人格、道德、审美的追求统摄
               起来，他基于道德又超越道德展现的是生命之美，如此之美是作为人之自我完善的过程中欲达到的精神高度，还可延伸至关于美
               的欣赏与创造。如何在当前社会环境下把握设计艺术之功利与人性之间的平衡，解决当代设计艺术“见欲不见性”“见文不见质”
              “见物不见人”等问题，从“充实之谓美”中或许能得到启示。
               关键词：孟子；美学；充实；设计艺术；自律
               Abstract: Fullness as Beauty is an important proposition of Mencius’ aesthetic thought. It is not only about the beauty of personality， but
               also about the pursuit of personality， morality and aesthetics. Based on morality and beyond morality， Mencius shows the beauty of life，
               which is the spiritual height to be achieved in the process of self-perfection， and can be extended to the appreciation and creation of beauty.
               It is important to keep balance between utilitarianism and humanity of design art in the current social environment and solve the problems
               such as "invisibility of desire"， "invisibility of literature" and "invisibility of human beings" in modern design. Fullness as Beauty may
               provide inspirations.
               Key words: Mencius；aesthetics；fullness；designing art；self-regulation
