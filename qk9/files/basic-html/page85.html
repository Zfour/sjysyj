                 15         壮族铜鼓元素在城市景观设计中的应用研究


                            Application of Bronze Drum Elements of Zhuang Nationality
                            in Urban Landscape Design





                            金旭明 JIN Xuming    徐沛文 XU Peiwen     严世华 YAN Shihua     吴隽洁 WU Junjie
                            桂林理工大学，广西桂林 541000（Guilin university of technology，541004 Guilin Guangxi）



                 摘要：将壮族铜鼓元素作为壮族文化的物化形态应用于城市景观设计中 ，丰富城市景观的多样性 。在彰显地域文化特色的同时 ，
                 传承民族文化和弘扬民族精神 。通过对壮族铜鼓中的装饰元素及该元素应用在城市景观设计中的实例分析 ，探究将铜鼓元素应用
                 到现代城市景观设计中的方法。
                 关键词： 铜鼓；壮族文化；景观设计
                 Abstract:  The Zhuang bronze drum elements are used in the urban landscape design as a materialized form of the Zhuang culture to
                 enrich the diversity of the urban landscape. While highlighting the characteristics of regional culture，it also inherited national culture and
                 promoted the national spirit. This article discusses the application of bronze drum elements in modern urban landscape design， methods and
                 cultural significance by discussing the decorative elements in the bronze drums of Zhuang nationality and the application of this element in
                 urban landscape design.
                 Key words: bronze drum；Zhuang culture；landscape design
