               18         林盘保护视域下的田园综合体景观设计研究


                          —— 以郫都区青杠树村为例


                          Study on Landscape Design of Pastoral Complex under the
                          View of Forest Protection： Taking Qinggangshu Village in
                          Pixian District as an Example



                          常玲敏 CHANG Lingmin
                          西南交通大学 , 成都 611756  （Southwest  Jiaotong University，611756 Chengdu）




               摘要：文章从林盘保护的角度出发，结合田园综合体的景观设计策略，探索当前社会背景下川西林盘发展的新思路。首先对林盘
               的概念及其设计元素进行了分析，然后就田园综合体的景观设计方法展开讨论，接着以郫都区青杠树村为例，探讨了林盘景观要
               素对田园综合体景观营造的影响，为林盘的保护及田园综合体的创新发展提供理论支撑。
               关键词：川西林盘；田园综合体；景观设计
               Abstract:  From the perspective of forest protection, combined with the landscape design strategy of the pastoral complex, this paper
               explores the new ideas of the development of the forest plate in western Sichuan under the current social background. Firstly, the concept
               of forest disk and its design elements are analyzed. Then the landscape design method of the pastoral complex is discussed. Then, the
               Qingbanshu Village in Qidu District is taken as an example to discuss the forest landscape elements to the pastoral complex and theimpact
               of landscape construction. The paper aims to provide theoretical support for the protection of forest discs and the innovative development
               ofrural complexes.
               Key words: western Sichuan forest plate；  pastoral complex；  landscape design
