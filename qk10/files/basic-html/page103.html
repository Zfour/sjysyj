               20         乡土卵石景观界面设计 *


                          Interface Design in Local Pebble-work Landscape





                          季茜 JI Qian    郑家瑜 ZHENG Jiayu
                          华中科技大学，湖北武汉 430074（Huazhong University of Science and Technology，430074
                          Wuhan Hubei）



               摘要：通过对浙闽两省的乡土卵石景观进行调研，旨在建立浙闽两省乡土卵石景观数据库。对浙闽两省调研资料进行存储及整合，
               研发乡土卵石景观交互设计系统，用界面与交互设计的理论方法将调研的样本资料进行直观的界面展示，构建可交互的乡土卵石
               景观分析研究工具，使乡土卵石景观研究系统有利于传播和发展。
               关键词：界面设计；交互系统；乡土卵石景观；浙闽两省
               Abstract: Through the investigation of the local pebble landscape in Zhejiang and Fujian provinces, this paper aims to establish thedatabase
               of the local pebble landscape in Zhejiang and Fujian provinces. This paper stores and integrates the investigation data of Zhejiangand Fujian
               provinces, develops the interactive design system of local pebble landscape, displays the sample data intuitively with thetheoretical method
               of interface and interactive design, and constructs an interactive analysis tool of local pebble landscape, which makes theresearch system of
               local pebble landscape propagate and develop easily.
               Key words: interface design；interactive system；local pebble-work landscape；Zhejiang and Fujian provinces
