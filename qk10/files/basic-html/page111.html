               21         城市轨道交通车辆车头造型设计中的表情认知

                          研究 *


                          Research on Expression Cognition in the Model Design of
                          Urban Rail Transit Vehicles




                          谢玄晖 XIE Xuanhui
                          西南交通大学，成都 610031（Southwest Jiaotong University , 610031 Chengdu）



               摘要：通过对国内外城市轨道交通车辆车头前脸表情设计的调研与分析，总结城市轨道交通车辆表情设计的构成要素集及其识别
               特点。对轨道交通车辆车头的造型要素分解，提取造型特征线，车脸表情组成元素，与人物面部表情进行类比，分析其表情认知
               过程的关键信息，实现车头造型设计的物理域与语义域之间的转换，建立城市轨道交通车辆车头前脸表情的认知模型，为提升我
               国城市轨道交通车辆装备设计的综合竞争力提供支持。
               关键词：轨道交通车辆；车头造型；表情设计；设计认知
               Abstract:  Based on the investigation and analysis of the facial expression design of urban rail transit vehicles at home and abroad, this
               paper summarizes the elements and identification features of facial expression design of urban rail transit vehicles. This paper decomposes
               the modeling elements of the rail transit vehicle head, extracts the modeling feature lines and the components of the facial expressions,
               and makes an analogy with the facial expressions of the characters. It also analyses the key information of the cognitive process of the
               facial expressions, realizes the transformation between the physical domain and the semantic domain of the head modeling design, and
               establishes the facial expressions of the urban rail transit vehicle head. Cognitive model provides support for improving the comprehensive
               competitiveness of urban rail transit vehicle equipment design in China.
               Key words: rail transit vehicles；head shape；Emoji design；design cognition
