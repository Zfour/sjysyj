               06         江南地域人文环境对本土油画的深层影响*


                          The Deep Influence of the Cultural Environment in Jiangnan
                          Regions on the Local Oil Painting






                          余方林 YU Fanglin
                          江南影视艺术职业学院，江苏无锡 214100（Jiangnan Vocational College of Media Arts，214100
                          Wuxi Jiangsu）




               摘要：意象图式的江南油画具有较强的地域文化性，它的发生、发展受到了江南自然地理环境、隐性的江南诗性文化精神和文人
               画传统，以及江南地域文化性格、审美心理等多方面因素的影响，它在现当代的发生、发展具有鲜明的独特性、地域性、传承性、
               时代性特点。从江南油画的题材的选择、艺术风格以及表现手法方面来考察地域人文环境对江南油画的影响，可以深层次地了解
               江南本土油画的历史和现状，有利于油画民族化、本土化探索，具有重要的现实意义。
               关键词：江南油画；江南地域文化性格；文人画传统；江南地域人文环境
               Abstract: The Jiangnan oil painting in image schema has a strong regional culture. Its occurrence and development have been influenced
               by the natural geographical environment, the recessive poetic cultural spirit and the literatipainting tradition, as well as the cultural character
               in Jiangnan regions and the esthetic psychology, and have the distinct characteristicsof uniqueness, territoriality, inheritance and time in the
               modern and contemporary era. This paper examines the impact of regional humanistic environment on the Jiangnan oil painting from the
               selection of the subject matter, the artistic style and the expression techniqueof the Jiangnan oil painting to deeply understand the history
               and the current situation of the local oil painting in Jiangnan regions. It is conducive to the nationalization and localization exploration of oil
               painting and has an important practical significance.
               Key  words:  Jiangnan Oil Painting；Cultural Character in Jiangnan Regions；Literati Painting Tradition；Humanistic Environment in
               JiangnanRegions
