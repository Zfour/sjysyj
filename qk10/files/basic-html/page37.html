               07         美国高校室内设计教学实践探讨


                          —— 以明尼苏达大学室内工作室课程为例


                          Discussion on Educational Practice of Interior Design in
                          American Universities: Taking the interior studio course of
                          UMN as an example


                          宋晓真 SONG Xiaozhen
                          苏州工艺美术职业技术学院，苏州215104 （Suzhou Art & Design Technology Institute , 215104
                          Suzhou )




               摘要：文章以笔者亲身调研与参与的明尼苏达大学二年级下学期室内工作室课程为例，通过教学项目的设置、教学内容的组织和
               教学效果的评价等方面的介绍，总结了对我国艺术设计教学的几点启示，即横向的跨学科设置课程、纵向的与企业对接教学以及
               运用灵活、现代化的教育技术手段和开放系统的教学互评体系，以期为我国的设计教育改革提供参考。
               关键词：室内设计；教学实践；工作室课程
               Abstract:  The University of Minnesota is one of the most prestigious and comprehensive universities in the United States. The interior
               design course of the School of Design is a professional course accredited by the American Interior Design Accreditation Council (CIDA). The
               article takes the author's personal research and participation in second semester of indoor studio course of the second year of the Universityof
               Minnesota as an example. By analyzing the course setting, contents organization and curriculum evaluation, the paper gets several important
               enlightenments for Art & Design education in China, including intercrossing different subjects horizontally, docking to enterprise, flexibly
               applying modern skills and opening evaluation system vertically, expecting to provide reference for China's designeducation reform.
               Key words: Interior Design；Educational Practice；Studio Course
