               12         儿童安防产品的情趣化设计研究


                          Delight-oriented Design of Children's Security Products







                          吴绍兰WU Shaolan    吴学晨WU Xuechen
                          福州大学厦门工艺美术学院，福建厦门 361024 （Xiamen institute of arts and crafts，
                          Fuzhouuniversity，361024 Xiamen Fujian）




               摘要：研究情趣化设计在儿童安防产品中的应用，降低儿童遭遇安全风险的隐患。分析儿童安全现状、儿童安防产品的目标用户
               特点，从情趣化设计理论出发，在造型、色彩、交互式体验等方面对儿童安防产品进行探究。儿童安防产品引入情趣化的设计理
               念不仅能够增强产品的趣味性，还能降低产品的功能辨识性，从而更好地保护儿童健康发展。
               关键词：儿童安全；情趣化设计；儿童安防产品
               Abstract:  The paper studies the application of delight-oriented design in children's security products to reduce the risk of children's
               exposure to safety risks. It analyzes the status quo of children's safety and the characteristics of target users of children's security products.
               Based on the theory delight-oriented Design，children's security products are analyzed in terms of modeling, color and interactive
               experience. The introduction of the child-friendly product design concept not only enhances the fun of the product, but also reduces the
               functional recognition of the product, thus better protecting the healthy development of children.
               Key words: child safety；delight-oriented design；children's security products
