               05         历史文化记忆的再生： 登琨艳对“老建筑”保护
                          性设计的思想研究*


                          The Regeneration of Historical and Cultural Memory: A
                          Study on the Thought of Protective Design of Old Buildings

                          by Deng Kunyan




                          代锋 Dai Feng
                          江苏科技大学，江苏镇江212003 （Jiangsu University of Science and Technology , 212003 Zhenjiang
                          Jiangsu）





               摘要：在城市化的进程中，虽然带有历史文化的老建筑能够引起人们的重视，但是由于各地方保护政策的不完善与保护态度的差
               异，老建筑的保护与再生仍然面临严峻的挑战。通过探析登琨艳对上海苏州河畔一栋具有历史文化的老建筑的保护性设计思想，
               呈现了中国传统文化在建筑空间情境营造上的一种可能，为中国历史文化建筑的保护与再生提供一种可借鉴的方式。
               关键词：登琨艳；老建筑；保护性设计；思想
               Abstract: In the process of rapid urban construction, though the historical and cultural old buildings can arouse people's attention, owing
               toits inadequate protection policy and different protection attitude, the protection and reconstruction of old buildings have been facingsevere
               challenges. By analyzing  a historical and cultural old building along Suzhou River in Shanghai, which is a protective design work by Deng
               Kunyan, it proposes a possibility to Chinese traditional culture in the construction of the building space, and provides a referenceto protection
               and reconstruction about Chinese historical and cultural architecture.
               Key words: Deng Kunyan；old buildings；Protective Design；thought
