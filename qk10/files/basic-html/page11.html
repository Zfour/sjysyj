               02         论设计的逻辑原理*


                          On the Logic Principle of Design






                          成乔明 CHENG Qiaoming
                          南京航空航天大学 ,  南京 210016（Nanjing University of Aeronautic and Astronautic , Nanjing
                          210016）



               摘要：设计是将记忆进行解构的过程与活动，这是设计思维的根源。解与构都深刻体现了设计师的情感转移，有意识情感转移是
               记忆的延续，模仿出了设计作品的经典形；无意识情感转移是记忆的突破，创造出了设计作品的奇异形。所谓设计的继承是对熟
               悉情感的迷恋，所谓设计的创新是对陌生情感的探奇。设计创新是有度的陌生，过度的陌生使人产生恐惧感，此时设计就又开始
               回归经典形。
               关键词：设计；逻辑原理；记忆；情感转移
               Abstract:  Design is a process and an activity of deconstructing, which is the root of design thinking. Both decomposition andreconstruction
               deeplyembody the emotional transfer of designer. On the one hand, the conscious emotion transfer is the continuation of the memory
               andimitates the classical form of the design work. On the other hand, the unconscious emotion transfer is the breakthrough of the memory
               and creates a novelshape. Design inheritance is the fascination with the familiar emotion and design innovation is the exploration of the novel
               emotion. Designinnovation is the strangeness with a certain degree. Excessive strangeness arouses fear. On this occasion, the design begins to
               return to the classicalform.
               Key words:  design；Logic Principle；memory；emotion transfer
