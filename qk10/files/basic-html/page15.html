               03         中国传统女性丝绸肩饰的审美特点及衍变*


                          On  the  Artistic  Characteristics  and  Development  of
                          Traditional Chinese Silk Shoulder Accessories for Female
                          Customers




                                                 1
                                                                       2
                          李建亮 LI Jianliang    王建芳 WANG Jianfang    杨格格 YANG Gege
                                1
                          1. 浙江理工大学，浙江杭州 310018（Zhejiang Sci-Tech University，310018 Hangzhou Zhejiang）
                          2. 东华大学，上海 201600（Donghua University，201600 Shanghai）


               摘要：从女性肩饰艺术的角度，分析帔帛、霞帔、云肩、丝巾等丝绸服饰品在造型、纹饰以及功用性能上的审美特点。在循序渐
               进的发展史中，探索女性丝绸肩饰艺术与传统服饰文化、社会审美转换、中西文化交流之间的内在关系，阐述其衍变过程中有益
               的设计经验，以及对实用性和装饰性两者之间度的把握，为当前丝绸产品的创新开发提供可循的文化根据和价值参考。
               关键词：女性；丝绸；肩部配饰；审美特点；衍变
               Abstract:  This paper summarizes the artistic characteristics of the silk shawl, silk cape, cloud shoulder, scarves in the shape, decoration
               and function, from perspective of female shoulder accessories. It explores the inherent relationship between the evolution ofthe art of female
               silk shoulder accessories and the traditional costume culture、aesthetic transformation of society as well as the culturalexchanges between
               China and the west . To sum up the important design experience in the development, and how to balance the practicalityand ornamentation;
               to provide areliable cultural basis and value for innovation and development of the current silk products.
               Key words:  female；silk；shoulder accessories； Aesthetic characteristic；development
