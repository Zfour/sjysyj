               17         基于情感体验的湛江特产品牌形象设计研究 *


                          Research on Brand Image Design of Zhanjiang Specialty
                          Products Based on Emotional Experience





                          徐伶俐 Xu Lingli     罗娜  Luo Na
                          广东海洋大学 , 广东湛江 524000（Guangdong Ocean University , 524000 Zhanjiang Guangdong）



               摘要：针对当下湛江特产品牌形象设计的现状与问题，笔者提出将情感体验融入湛江特产品牌形象设计之中，最大限度地发挥湛
               江特产品牌形象所具有的文化内涵，从而将其内在气质调动出来，形成独特的品牌文化，让消费者购买到的不仅仅是特产本身，
               而且还能享受到愉快的情感体验过程。恰当的品牌形象设计能够让更多的消费者与品牌建立起体验情感，这种情感的联系将成为
               消费者认可特产品牌个性、支持特产品牌的保证。
               关键词：情感体验；湛江特产；品牌形象设计
               Abstract: Considering the current status quo and problems of Zhanjiang specialty products brand image design, it is proposed to integrate
               theemotional experience into the brand image design of Zhanjiang special products, which maximizes the expression of cultural connotation
               of Zhanjiang specialty products brand image, mobilizes its internal temperament and forms a uniquebrand culture. In addition to buying
               the specialty itself, consumer can also enjoya pleasant emotional experience. Moreover, appropriate brand image design can encourage
               consumersand brands to establish experience emotions. This kinds of emotional tie will guarantee the consumers to recognize the special
               product brandpersonality and support the special product brand.
               Key words: emotional experience；Zhanjiang specialty；brand image design
