               19         博物馆展示空间灯光设计研究



                          Research on The Lighting design of Museum Exhibition
                          Space





                          杨慧 YANG Hui    马云林 MA Yunlin
                          昆明理工大学 , 昆明 650500（Kunming University of Science and Technology , Kunming 650500）




               摘要：衡量现代博物馆室内展示空间水平的一项重要指标就是其光环境运用的合理性。因此，灯光设计的研究是博物馆展示空间
               整体设计过程中不可忽视的首要问题。通过对博物馆展示空间灯光设计的研究，分析不同形式的灯光所呈现的艺术效果及其产生
               的影响，在此基础上探讨灯光设计在博物馆空间展陈设计中的应用原则和方法，为创造符合参观者认知行为且能够体现展品文化
               内涵的展示空间提供理论依据。
               关键词：博物馆；展示空间；灯光设计
               Abstract: An important indicator of indoor display space level in modern museums is the rationality of the light environment. Therefore,the
               research of lighting design is the primary issue that cannot be ignored in the overall design process of the museum display space. Through
               the research on the lighting design of the museum display space, this paper analyzes the artistic effects and influences of different forms of
               lighting. On this basis, it discusses the application principles and methods of lighting design in the museum display space design. It provides
               a theoretical basis for creating a display space that is in line with the cognitive behavior of visitors and can reflect the cultural connotation
               ofexhibits.
               Key words: museum；exhibition space；lighting design
